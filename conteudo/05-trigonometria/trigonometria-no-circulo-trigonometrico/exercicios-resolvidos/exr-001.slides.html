(ACAFE 2021)   O nmero de solues da equao $\,2\cos^2{x} - \text{sen}\,{x} = 1\,$ no intervalo $\,[0,2\pi]\,$

a) 2
b) 1
c) 3
d) nenhum

Pelo Teorema Fundamental da Trigonometria, $$ \text{sen}^2\,{x} + \cos^2{x} = 1 \implies \cos^2{x} = \boxed{\,1 - \text{sen}^2\,{x}\,} $$

$$ 2(1 - \text{sen}^2\,{x}) - \text{sen}\,{x} - 1 = 0 $$
$$ 2 - 2\,\text{sen}^2\,{x} - \text{sen}\,{x} - 1 = 0 $$
$$ -2\,\text{sen}^2\,{x} - \text{sen}\,{x} + 1 = 0 \\[12pt] 2\,\text{sen}^2{x} + \text{sen}\,{x} - 1 = 0 $$

Fazendo $m = \text{sen}\,{x}$,

$$ 2\,m^2 + m - 1 = 0 $$

Pela Frmula de "Bhaskara",

$$ m = \cfrac{-1\pm\sqrt{1^2-4\cdot 2\cdot (-1)}}{2\cdot 2} \\ $$
$$ m = \cfrac{-1\pm\sqrt{9}}{4} = \cfrac{-1\pm 3}{4} \\[10pt] $$
$$ m = \cfrac{2}{4} = \cfrac{1}{2} \quad\text{ou}\quad m = \cfrac{-4\,}{4} = -1 $$

Devemos lembrar que: $$ m = \text{sen}\,x $$

$$ \text{sen}\,x = \cfrac{1}{\,2} \quad\text{ou}\quad \text{sen}\,x = -1 $$

$$ X_1 = \text{arcsen}\,\cfrac{1}{\,2} = \left\{\cfrac{\pi}{6}\,,\cfrac{5\pi}{6}\right\} \\[10pt] X_2 = \text{arcsen}\,-1 = \left\{\cfrac{3\pi}{2}\right\} $$
