## Sistemas de 2 equações e duas incógnitas

* [exr-2025-04-30](./exercicios-resolvidos/exr-2025-04-30.ipynb)

$$
\left\lbrace
  \begin{align*}
  \ x \ + \ y \ & = \ 810 \\
  \ \operatorname{mdc}(x, y) \ & = \ 45
  \end{align*}
\right.
$$

$$
\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
\ x \ + \ y \ & = \ 810 \\
\ mdc(x, y) \ & = \ 4 \\
\end{split}
\end{equation*}
$$
